In the bonus task, you start with a tentative bonus of ${{ C.START_VALUE }}. You will then open {{ C.NUM_FORCED_OPEN }} boxes. Each time you open a box, you will wait {{ C.DELAY }} seconds to reveal the bonus inside. One of two things can happen to your tentative bonus after you open a box:

  1. If the bonus inside the box is smaller than your tentative bonus, your tentative bonus will stay the same.
  2. If the bonus inside the box is larger than your tentative bonus, the bonus inside the box will become your new tentative bonus.
After you open all {{ C.NUM_FORCED_OPEN }} boxes, the final tentative bonus will be added to your payment from Part 1.
