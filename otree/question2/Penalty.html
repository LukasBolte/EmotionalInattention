While you work on the bonus task, the computer will complete a penalty task.

In the penalty task, the computer start with a tentative penalty of ${{ C.END_VALUE }}. It will then open {{ C.NUM_FORCED_OPEN }} boxes. Each time it opens a box, it will wait {{ C.DELAY }} seconds to reveal the penalty inside. Two things can happen to the computer's tentative payment after it opens a box:

  1. If the penalty inside the box is larger than its tentative penalty, its tentative penalty stays the same.
  2. If the penalty inside the box is smaller than its tentative penalty, the penalty inside the box becomes its new tentative penalty.
After the computer opens all {{ C.NUM_FORCED_OPEN }} boxes, the final tentative penalty will be taken away from your payment from Part 1.
