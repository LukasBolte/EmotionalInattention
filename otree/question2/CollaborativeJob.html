The Collaborative Job consists of two tasks: a bonus task and a penalty task.

Suppose that Part 1 is chosen for payment and you decide to complete the Collaborative Job. Then you will complete the bonus task, and a computer will complete the penalty task. You will receive the payoff earned by you AND the computer.
